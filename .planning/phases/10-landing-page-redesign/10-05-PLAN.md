## Objective

## Reference Analysis

### Testimonials
- Minimal styling (no heavy cards)
- Quote + author + role
- Sometimes inline with feature sections
- Photo optional but nice to have

### CTA Section
- Clean, not overly heavy gradient
- Simple headline and CTA button
- Subtle background differentiation
- No bullet lists inside CTA
