## Objective
Redesign social proof to use text-only brand names (no cards), and stats section to use large numbers with geometric triangle markers (no cards/boxes).

## Reference Analysis

### Social Proof (Brand Logos)
- Simple text: "Loved by brands"
- Brand names as text (like "TOZO", "cocokind", "Oxyfresh")
- Horizontal row, no boxes/cards
- Grayscale/muted appearance
- Some have stylized fonts, but we'll use consistent font

### Stats Section
- Large numbers: "6 million", "31%", "29 million"
- Small colored triangle markers before each stat
- Minimal descriptive text below
- No cards or boxes around stats
- Clean white background
